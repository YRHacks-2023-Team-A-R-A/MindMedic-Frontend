⠀MindMedic

MindMedic is a multi-purpose health themed discord bot. Its main defining feature is its therapist/doctor AI hence the name "MindMedic". With a simple command you can start a session with the AI and talk to it about about anything from the cause of a headache to recalling a traumatic event from the past. It also has various other features like being able to check how much calories are in a type of food or knowing how many COVID-19 cases are active at the moment.

Try asking the MindMedic AI some questions!
